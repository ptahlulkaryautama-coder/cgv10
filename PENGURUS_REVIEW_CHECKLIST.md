# Pengurus Review Checklist

This checklist lists the official information still needed before CGV10 is shared more widely with residents.

## Required Approvals

- Approved pengurus names and roles.
- Approved financial summary format.
- Approved announcement categories.
- Approved official contact numbers.
- Approved QR codes, if needed.
- PALUGADA seller and item submission rules.
- PALUGADA item approval flow.
- Public launch approval from pengurus.

## Content To Validate

- Quick-info labels and wording for saldo kas, jumlah KK, pengaduan status, and kegiatan terdekat.
- Public wording for pengurus, keuangan, pengumuman, PALUGADA, and kontak pages.
- Logo usage for CGV10, Cipta Greenville, RT 010 / RW 021, and PALUGADA CGV.
- Placeholder language that should remain visible until official data is approved.

## Publication Boundaries

- Do not publish private names, phone numbers, QR codes, financial values, PALUGADA item data, or seller details before approval.
- Keep PALUGADA CGV as a promotion and directory campaign until official transaction rules are approved.
- Keep the website as a static public Phase 1 portal; no login, payment, cart, database, or active transaction flow is included.
